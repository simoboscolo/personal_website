Mylk

Milk branding and packaging design.

Mylk was a school project aimed to develop the packaging for a bottle of milk.

As there was no limitation in the brief I developed a modern, clear and simple design to fit the product.

The simple design makes the bottle recognizable and evocates the product.

The bottles are stackable and close trough a clip positioned in the lid and a groove at mid-height.
