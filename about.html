simone boscolo

Graphic, web, illustration.

I studied Graphic Design, History of Arts, Cinema and Photography at the Italian State Institute of Arts. After my studies I went to London to study English and then I moved to Amsterdam. I believe the incredible mix of cultures I've met during my first period here influenced, changed and improved me even more then I could possibly explain.

I am very enthusiastic about anything that interests me and stimulates my curiosity. I am very organised and methodic but very flexible when it comes to work in a team. I love travelling and art, having new experiences, creating new memories and meeting new people.

Cheers!
