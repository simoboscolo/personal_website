Aroma Plaza

Brand Identity, Webdesign, Illustration and Social Media

Aroma Plaza is located in Amsterdam and is the biggest home fragrance shop in The Netherlands.

Working on this project i had the unique opportunity to develop a complete new brand identity for the shop. Previously called Fragancias the business decided to relaunch his identity using a fun and clear design, showing the simplicity of the products they offer. Attention to the details played a very important part in the procedure and, having absolute liberty, I really could be creative, creating a consistent identity throughout Social Media, Shops and Webshop.

Buying online or in store people will collect 'Scenties' that can be exchanged for a discount.

Webshop and Social Media are updated and designed simultaneously for a uniform experience.

With any online order people will receive an hand-written 'Thank You!' card.
